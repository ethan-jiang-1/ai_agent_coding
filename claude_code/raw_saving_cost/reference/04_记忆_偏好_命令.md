## 关键摘录

### Claude Code 记忆

- “Each Claude Code session begins with a fresh context window.”
- “CLAUDE.md files are markdown files that give Claude persistent instructions for a project, your personal workflow, or your entire organization.”
- “CLAUDE.md files are loaded into the context window at the start of every session, consuming tokens alongside your conversation.”
- “Size: target under 200 lines per CLAUDE.md file.”
- “CLAUDE.md fully survives compaction. After /compact, Claude re-reads your CLAUDE.md from disk and re-injects it fresh into the session.”

### 个性化偏好

- “Profile preferences are account-wide settings that help Claude understand your general preferences that Claude should consider in responses.”
- “Any preferences you add here will be applied to all of your conversations with Claude.”
- “Project instructions help Claude understand the specific context and requirements for a particular project.”

### 命令

- slash commands 文档列出了 `/memory`、`/model` 等命令。
- 模型配置文档说明：`/model` 可立即切换当前会话模型，`/status` 可检查当前模型。

### 提示词清晰度

- “Just as when you instruct a human for the first time on a task, the more you explain exactly what you want in a straightforward manner, the better and more accurate Claude's response will be.”
- “When instructions are vague or open-ended, Claude may provide a more general response.”

## 对本文可用的结论

- 常用偏好不要每次会话重讲，应该沉淀到 Profile preferences、Project instructions 或 CLAUDE.md。
- `/compact` 适合长会话降噪；真正要跨会话长期保留的规则，应该写进 CLAUDE.md。
- 用户表达越清楚、越完整，追问越少，总轮次越低，成本自然更低。
